When Parafin cannot generate any designs that satisfy your current combination of filters and assumptions, a **No Results Available** message appears in the project panel. The message explains why generation produced zero results and lists specific things to try.

</Frame>

## What the message tells you

The surface lists the most common reasons and provides a button for each one to take you directly to the relevant setting:

- **The selected site may be too small**: click **Site Boundaries** to expand the site
- **Parking ratio may exceed site capacity**: click **Parking** to adjust the parking ratio
- **Program filters may be too stringent**: click **Filters** to widen or disable constraint filters
- **Zoning limits restricting height, FAR, or open space may be too stringent**: click **Zoning** to relax zoning-derived constraints
- **Selected brand-specific amenities or layouts may exclude all variants**. Consider switching to a different brand or adjusting non-guestroom program options

<Tip>
  Start with fewer constraints and add them incrementally. This makes it easier to identify which specific constraint is preventing results.
</Tip>
