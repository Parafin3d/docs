The last step before generating designs is choosing a hotel brand. The **Select a Hotel Brand** screen displays all available brands in a grid of cards. Each card shows the brand logo, name, and hotel family.

  <img src="/images/help/launching/brand-dialog.png" alt="Brand selection screen showing brand grid with family filter and hotel family icons" />
</Frame>

## Filtering brands

Three controls at the top of the screen help you narrow the list:

- **Chain Scale**: a dropdown that filters brands by hotel family (e.g., Hilton, Marriott, Choice Hotels, IHG). Family logos appear as icons in a row below the dropdown.
- **Extended Stay**: filters to extended-stay brands only
- **Generative**: toggles between fully generative brands (AI-generated layouts, budgets, and proformas) and prototype-only brands (conceptual layouts for feasibility studies). Generative brands are sorted before prototype-only brands.

## Selecting a brand

Click a brand card to select it. Then click **Generate Designs** at the bottom of the screen to start generation.

<Tip>
  You can change the brand later from the project dashboard. Adjust financial assumptions while Parafin generates so results reflect your numbers the moment they land.
</Tip>

For the current list of brands on Parafin, see [Brands](/brands/overview).
