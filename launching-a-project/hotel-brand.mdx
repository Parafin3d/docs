The last step before generating designs is choosing a hotel brand. The **Select a Hotel Brand** screen displays all available brands in a grid of cards. Each card shows the brand logo, name, hotel family, and a **Generative** or **Prototype-only** tag.

## Filtering brands

Two controls at the top of the screen help you narrow the list:

- **Generative**: turn on to show only fully generative brands (those with AI-generated layouts, budgets, and proformas). When off (the default), all brands appear, with generative brands sorted ahead of prototype-only brands.
- **Hotel family row**: a row of family logos (e.g., Hilton, Marriott, Choice Hotels, IHG). Click a family to filter the grid to brands in that family. Click it again to clear the filter.

## Selecting a brand

Click a brand card to select it. Then click **Generate Designs** at the bottom of the screen to start generation.

<Tip>
  You can change the brand later from the project dashboard. Adjust financial assumptions while Parafin generates so results reflect your numbers the moment they land.
</Tip>

For the current list of brands on Parafin, see [Brands](/brands/overview).
