<Update label="1.61" description="December 30, 2025" tags={["feature", "zoning"]}>
  ## Zoning Integration

  We've integrated zoning data directly into the Parafin workflow for sites across the United States and Canada.

  - **New Zoning Panel** — district codes, dimensional controls, and source links in one place. [Learn more →](/tutorials/zoning-panel)
  - **Zoning Filters** — apply FAR, height, and open space constraints with a single click
  - **Setbacks with Zoning** — pull suggested front, side, and rear setbacks from zoning data
  - **Project Creation Progress Bar** — clearer context as you move through project setup

  Zoning data is sourced from Zoneomics and refreshed weekly. Coverage spans the US and Canada.
</Update>

<Update label="1.59" description="November 18, 2025" tags={["improvement", "variants"]}>
  ## Variant generation improvements

  Variant generation is now significantly faster and produces more diverse layouts on complex parcels.

  - 40% faster generation on parcels with mixed setback requirements
  - New "compact" variant style for tight urban infill sites
  - Improved handling of irregular parcel shapes
</Update>
