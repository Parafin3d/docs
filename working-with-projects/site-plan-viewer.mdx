The Site Plan Viewer shows the selected design from above. Dimensions update live as you rotate or move the building — check setbacks and site geometry without leaving the view.

</Frame>

## Viewer toolbar and controls

The same toolbar (top-left) and navigation controls (bottom-right) that live on the [3D Building Viewer](/working-with-projects/3d-viewer) are available here — undo/redo, mirror building, edit boundaries and setbacks, snapshot, Street View / 3D Satellite toggle, re-center, north arrow, and zoom.
