<Update label="1.61" description="December 30, 2025" tags={["feature", "zoning", "pcf"]}>
  ## Zoning Integration

  Zoning data is now integrated directly into the Parafin workflow for sites across the United States and Canada. Coverage is free for all Parafin users.

  ### New Zoning Panel

  The **Zoning Panel** collects every available zoning detail for your site in one place. Open it by clicking anywhere in the project information section and expanding the **Zoning** section. You'll see the district code, description, zoning type, a direct link to the municipal zoning source, and the key dimensional controls that drive feasibility.

  <Frame caption="The new Zoning Panel with district code, zoning type, source link, and dimensional controls">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.61/zoning_panel.gif" alt="Walkthrough of the new Zoning Panel inside a project" />
  </Frame>

  [Learn more →](/tutorials/zoning-panel)

  ### Zoning Filters

  Apply FAR, maximum building height, and minimum open space constraints to your project with a single click. Each constraint displays a suggested zoning value — apply it as-is, enter your own value using the text field or slider, or turn the control off for that metric. Changes update your project assumptions in real time, and variant generations adjust accordingly.

  <Frame caption="Applying FAR, height, and open-space constraints directly from the zoning panel">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.61/control_filters.gif" alt="Toggling and applying Zoning Filters in the panel" />
  </Frame>

  ### Defining Setbacks with Zoning

  When zoning data is available for your site, Parafin displays suggested zoning values for front, side, and rear setbacks. Apply them as-is or adjust using the slider or text field; the site footprint in the viewer updates in real time.

  <Frame caption="Suggested zoning setback values applied to front, side, and rear">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.61/setback_define.gif" alt="Defining setbacks using suggested zoning values" />
  </Frame>

  Assign each labeled boundary to front, rear, or side yards. Parafin applies the matching zoning setback value to each designated edge.

  <Frame caption="Assigning labeled setback values to each edge of the site">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.61/setback_sides.gif" alt="Assigning setback values to labeled site edges" />
  </Frame>

  ### Zoning Data Coverage

  Zoning data is sourced from Zoneomics and refreshed weekly, covering the United States and Canada. If your site doesn't show zoning data, [let us know](mailto:support@parafin.ai) and we'll add it in the next weekly update.

  <Frame caption="Zoneomics coverage across the United States and Canada">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.61/zoneomics_coverage.jpg" alt="Map of Zoneomics zoning data coverage" />
  </Frame>

  ### Project Creation Progress Bar

  A new progress bar shows where you are in project creation. It reflects each major step — location, site definition, setbacks, and brand selection.

  <Frame caption="New project creation progress bar">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.61/project_progress_bar.png" alt="Project creation progress bar showing each step of setup" />
  </Frame>
</Update>
