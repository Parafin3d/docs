---
title: "Edit your account"
description: "Update your name, email address, and company name from the user menu."
---

Open the user menu in the **top-right corner** of the screen to access your profile and account settings.

<Frame caption="User profile and settings in the top-right corner">
  <img src="/images/releases/1.57/user_menu.gif" alt="Opening the user menu from the top-right corner" />
</Frame>

From the account settings panel you can update:

- First name
- Last name
- Email address
- Company name

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/support/legacy/getting-started/edit-user-account.png" alt="Account settings panel" />
</Frame>

Changes save immediately. To update your password, see [Change your password](/getting-started/changing-password).
