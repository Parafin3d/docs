## Editing setbacks later

You don't have to get setbacks right the first time. After the project is created, edit them from the **Setbacks** panel — open it from the setbacks button in the top toolbar, or by clicking anywhere in the project information side panel. The panel also lets you group adjacent sides together to simplify setback labeling on complex parcels. See the [1.59 release notes](/changelog) for details.
