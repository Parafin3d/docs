---
title: "Define the site area"
description: "Select parcels on the map to define the area of your opportunity."
---

Click parcels on the map to add them to your site. Click a parcel again to remove it. Zoom in or out to confirm you're selecting the right geometry.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/support/legacy/launching/project-site-area.jpg" alt="Selecting parcels on the map" />
</Frame>

<Note>
  Parcels must be adjacent. If your opportunity spans non-adjacent parcels, Parafin will not let you proceed.
</Note>

If the parcel geometry doesn't match your site exactly, [draw a custom boundary](/launching-a-project/drawing-site-area) instead.
