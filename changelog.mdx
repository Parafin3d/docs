<Update label="1.62 (preview)" tags={["coming soon", "feature", "improvement", "pcf", "zoning"]}>
  ## Redesigned project creation

  Integrating zoning data meant rethinking what belongs in project creation. The result is a cleaner four-step setup (name, location, site boundaries, brand) followed by **Generate Designs**, with one unified interface that covers creating a project, editing a site, and changing the brand. Setbacks are configured from the project dashboard, where you have the full zoning context.

  <Frame caption="A walkthrough of the new project creation flow">
    <video autoPlay muted loop playsInline className="w-full aspect-video" src="/images/releases/1.62/pcf_walkthrough.mp4" />
  </Frame>

  [Learn how to launch a project →](/launching-a-project/overview)

  ## New brand selection screen

  The last step of project creation now opens a full-screen brand grid. Browse every available brand at a glance, filter by hotel family, or turn on the **Generative** filter to show only fully generative brands. The same screen appears when you change brands on an existing project.

  <Frame caption="Browsing, filtering, and selecting a brand">
    <video autoPlay muted loop playsInline className="w-full aspect-video" src="/images/releases/1.62/brand_dialog.mp4" />
  </Frame>

  [See how brand selection works →](/launching-a-project/hotel-brand)

  ## Zoning awareness and design constraints

  Two changes to how zoning and filtering work together.

  ### Zoning awareness

  When zoning data is available for your site, a notification card appears in the project panel. Review it right away or dismiss it and come back later via the **Zoning** button.

  <Frame caption="Zoning awareness card in the project panel">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.62/zoning_awareness.png" alt="Zoning awareness card showing district code and a link to the Zoning Panel" />
  </Frame>

  ### Constraints, with zoning-aware limits

  What used to be **All Filters** is now **Constraints**, opened as the **Design Constraints** side sheet — same controls for building height, open space, room mix, and more, with a clearer name. When zoning data is available, building height and open space show the zoning baseline: you can set stricter values but not more lenient ones, and checkmarks next to each field confirm compliance.

  <Frame caption="The Design Constraints side sheet">
    <video autoPlay muted loop playsInline className="w-full aspect-video" src="/images/releases/1.62/design_constraints_sidesheet.mp4" />
  </Frame>

  [Explore constraints and zoning →](/working-with-projects/constraints-sorting)

  ## Understanding "no results"

  When none of the generated variants fit within your current constraints, a new message explains why and points you to the specific setting to adjust — site boundaries, parking, filters, or zoning limits.

  <Frame caption="No-results surface explaining why variants were excluded">

  </Frame>

  [Learn more about no-results guidance →](/working-with-projects/no-results)
</Update>

<Update label="1.61" description="December 30, 2025" tags={["feature", "zoning", "pcf"]}>
  ## Zoning Integration

  Zoning data is now integrated directly into the Parafin workflow for sites across the United States and Canada. Coverage is free for all Parafin users.

  ### New Zoning Panel

  The **Zoning Panel** collects every available zoning detail for your site in one place. Open it by clicking anywhere in the project information section and expanding the **Zoning** section. You'll see the district code, description, zoning type, a direct link to the municipal zoning source, and the key dimensional controls that drive feasibility.

  <Frame caption="The new Zoning Panel with district code, zoning type, source link, and dimensional controls">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.61/zoning_panel.gif" alt="Walkthrough of the new Zoning Panel inside a project" />
  </Frame>

  [Learn more →](/tutorials/zoning-panel)

  ### Zoning Filters

  Apply FAR, maximum building height, and minimum open space constraints to your project with a single click. Each constraint displays a suggested zoning value — apply it as-is, enter your own value using the text field or slider, or turn the control off for that metric. Changes update your project assumptions in real time, and variant generations adjust accordingly.

  <Frame caption="Applying FAR, height, and open-space constraints directly from the zoning panel">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.61/control_filters.gif" alt="Toggling and applying Zoning Filters in the panel" />
  </Frame>

  ### Defining Setbacks with Zoning

  When zoning data is available for your site, Parafin displays suggested zoning values for front, side, and rear setbacks. Apply them as-is or adjust using the slider or text field; the site footprint in the viewer updates in real time.

  <Frame caption="Suggested zoning setback values applied to front, side, and rear">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.61/setback_define.gif" alt="Defining setbacks using suggested zoning values" />
  </Frame>

  Assign each labeled boundary to front, rear, or side yards. Parafin applies the matching zoning setback value to each designated edge.

  <Frame caption="Assigning labeled setback values to each edge of the site">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.61/setback_sides.gif" alt="Assigning setback values to labeled site edges" />
  </Frame>

  ### Zoning Data Coverage

  Zoning data is sourced from Zoneomics and refreshed weekly, covering the United States and Canada. If your site doesn't show zoning data, [let us know](mailto:support@parafin.ai) and we'll add it in the next weekly update.

  <Frame caption="Zoneomics coverage across the United States and Canada">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.61/zoneomics_coverage.jpg" alt="Map of Zoneomics zoning data coverage" />
  </Frame>

  ### Project Creation Progress Bar

  A new progress bar shows where you are in project creation. It reflects each major step — location, site definition, setbacks, and brand selection.

  <Frame caption="New project creation progress bar">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.61/project_progress_bar.png" alt="Project creation progress bar showing each step of setup" />
  </Frame>
</Update>

<Update label="1.52" description="March 7, 2025" tags={["feature", "improvement", "editor"]}>
  ## LARC GIS, RaaP for Hyatt Studios, and viewer improvements

  Location-based RevPAR estimates from LARC GIS land in the project dashboard, RaaP construction cost estimates extend to Hyatt Studios, and the viewer expands.

  ### LARC GIS RevPAR estimates (beta pilot)

  LARC GIS is a hotel-industry geographic information system from Lodging Analytics Research & Consulting that tracks lodging market conditions, supply, and revenue forecasts. Toggle **Use LARC Estimate** in the project dashboard to incorporate LARC's location-based RevPAR estimate into the investment model, and apply an index to adjust the estimate.

  <Frame caption="Toggle Use LARC Estimate to incorporate LARC's location-based RevPAR into the investment model">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.52/larc_revpar_toggle.png" alt="LARC RevPAR estimate toggle in the project dashboard" />
  </Frame>

  ### RaaP construction cost estimates for Hyatt Studios

  Hyatt Studios is the latest brand with [RaaP](https://www.raap.builders/parafin) construction cost estimates available in Parafin. RaaP estimates are tuned to brand-specific materials and finishes and break out costs in CSI MasterFormat® divisions. Previously available for Home2 Suites only.

  <Frame caption="RaaP divisional CSI MasterFormat® cost breakdown">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.52/raap_divisional.png" alt="RaaP divisional cost breakdown for a Hyatt Studios project" />
  </Frame>

  Toggle **RaaP Cost Estimate** on a Hyatt Studios project to switch from $/gsf or $/key estimates to detailed RaaP cost estimates. Pilot access is invite-only.

  ### Expanded viewer

  The viewer expands to use as much screen space as possible. Collapse the variant details window using the tab to expand even further.

  <Frame caption="The viewer expanded to fill the available screen space">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.52/expanded_viewer.png" alt="The expanded viewer with the variant details panel collapsed" />
  </Frame>

  ### Improved viewer interaction

  New controls for camera angle and zoom:

  - **Recenter** the view on the building
  - **Snapshot** tool to capture the current view for download or PDF inclusion
  - **Info button** that lists keyboard and mouse controls
  - **Map style toggle** between satellite imagery and a minimalist map view

  <Frame caption="Refreshed viewer interaction controls including recenter, snapshot, info, and map toggle">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.52/viewer_interaction.png" alt="Refreshed viewer interaction controls" />
  </Frame>

  ### Dedicated export window

  Clicking the download button in the Variants window now opens a dedicated export window with four export options. Past exports remain accessible from the folder icon.

  <Frame caption="The new export dialog with four export options">
