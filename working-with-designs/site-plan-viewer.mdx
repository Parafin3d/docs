---
title: "Site Plan Viewer"
description: "Top-down view of the selected building with live dimensioning."
---

The Site Plan Viewer is the top-down counterpart to the 3D Building Viewer. Dimensions update live as you rotate or move the building, so you can check setbacks and site geometry without leaving the view.
