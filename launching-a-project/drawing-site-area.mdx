---
title: "Draw the site area"
description: "Draw a custom boundary when parcel geometry doesn't match your site."
---

When the available parcels don't match the actual shape of your site, draw the boundary by hand.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Draw New Shape">
    Choose **Draw New Shape** from the site area tools.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add vertices">
    Click each corner of your site to place a vertex.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Double-click to finish">
    Double-click the final vertex to close the shape.
  </Step>
</Steps>
