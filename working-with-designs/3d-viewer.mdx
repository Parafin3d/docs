---
title: "3D Building Viewer"
description: "Rotate, mirror, and reposition the selected building on its site."
---

Move the selected design in place on its site:

- **Rotate** around the site center
- **Mirror** across a chosen axis
- **Translate** to reposition within the site

Property constraints turn red when violated — you see issues the moment they happen.
