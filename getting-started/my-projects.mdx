title: "My Projects and archived projects"
description: "Access your project dashboard, archive completed work, and retrieve archived projects."
---

The **My Projects** dashboard is your landing page after signing in. Every project you've created or been added to shows up here.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/support/legacy/getting-started/project-dashboard.png" alt="My Projects dashboard" />
</Frame>

## Archive a project

Click the triple-dot menu on any project card and choose **Archive**. Archived projects move out of your active list but stay retrievable.

## View archived projects

Click **View Archived Projects** in the top left of the dashboard to browse everything you've archived. Restore or permanently delete from there.
