---
title: "Variant details"
description: "Explore floorplans, tables, diagrams, and program options for the selected design variant."
---

Select any variant in the ranking list to load its details in the middle panel. Everything in this panel — floorplans, tables, diagrams, and program options — updates when you switch variants.

## Floorplans

Floorplan previews sit at the top of the middle panel. Click any floor number to load that floor's plan. Use the expand icon to view it fullscreen.

## Exporting a variant

Click the download button in the Variants window to open the export dialog. Four export options are available. Past exports stay accessible from the folder icon.
