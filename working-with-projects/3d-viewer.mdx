---

The 3D Building Viewer lets you move the selected design in place on its site and see how it responds to site constraints.

## Viewer toolbar

The viewer toolbar sits in the **top-left corner** and centralizes the key design actions:

- **Undo / redo** recent changes
- **Mirror building** across a chosen axis
- **Edit site boundaries and setbacks** without leaving the viewer
- **Create a snapshot** of the current view for reports or exports

## Viewer controls

Navigation controls live in the **bottom-right corner** of the viewer:

- **Toggle** between Street View and 3D Satellite view
- **Re-center** your site in the viewer
- **North arrow** to orient your bearings
- **Zoom** in and out

## Constraint warnings

Property constraints turn red when violated — you see issues the moment they happen as you rotate or reposition the building.
