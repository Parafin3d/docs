title: "Brands on Parafin"
description: "Hotel brands available in Parafin, their generative status, and how to request additions."
---

Parafin generates brand-compliant designs for a growing list of hotel brands. Each brand falls into one of three states:

| Status | What it means |
| --- | --- |
| **Live** | Fully generative — Parafin produces brand-compliant layouts, budgets, and proformas on demand |
| **Training** | Active model training in progress; preview designs may be available |
| **Prototype-only** | Fixed prototype designs available for feasibility studies; full generative support planned |

<Note>
  A dedicated, filterable brand registry page with every supported brand is planned for a future release. The section below documents notable brand additions as they shipped. For the most current state of any specific brand, contact your brand development representative (shown in the lower right when a project is open) or email [support@parafin.ai](mailto:support@parafin.ai).
</Note>
