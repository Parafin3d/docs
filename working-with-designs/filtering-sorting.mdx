Parafin generates thousands of candidate designs per site. Filters narrow the set and sort controls surface the best options for the metrics that matter to you.

## Filters in the Project Panel

## What you can filter on

- FAR
- Building height
- Number of keys
- Room mix
- Program elements

When zoning data is available for your site, Parafin also suggests zoning-derived values for FAR, maximum building height, and minimum open space. Apply any of them as-is or override with your own value — see the [Zoning Panel tutorial](/tutorials/zoning-panel) for how the zoning filters work.

## Sorting designs

Sort the design list by any financial metric:

- Number of keys
- Budget per key
- Equity multiple
- Yield

  <img src="/images/support/legacy/designs/sorting-designs.jpg" alt="Sort controls and sorted design list" />

## Star and hide

Click the star icon on any design to pin it to the top of the list regardless of sort. Click the **X** to hide a design from the list entirely.
