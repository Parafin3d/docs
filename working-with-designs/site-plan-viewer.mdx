---
title: "Site Plan Viewer"
description: "Top-down view of the selected building with live dimensioning."
---

The Site Plan Viewer shows the selected design from above. Use the **viewer toolbar** in the top-left to rotate, mirror, or translate the building; navigation controls are in the **bottom-right**. Dimensions update live as you move the building — check setbacks and site geometry without leaving the view.
