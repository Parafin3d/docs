When you start a new project, a **Create Your Project** screen previews all the steps before you begin. Click **Get Started** to begin.

<Frame caption="Create Your Project preview screen">
  <img src="/images/help/launching/step-preview.png" alt="Create Your Project screen showing all steps with Get Started button" />
</Frame>

Once you click **Get Started**, a list of steps on the left side of the screen tracks where you are in the flow. Completed steps show a checkmark. Click any completed step to go back and make changes.

## The four steps

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="1. Project name" href="/launching-a-project/project-name">
    Give your project a name. You can change it later.
  </Card>
  <Card title="2. Location" href="/launching-a-project/site-address">
    Find where in the world your project is located.
  </Card>
  <Card title="3. Site boundaries" href="/launching-a-project/site-area">
    Combine parcels or draw the boundaries of your site.
  </Card>
  <Card title="4. Brand" href="/launching-a-project/hotel-brand">
    Choose a hotel brand. You can run Parafin with other brands later.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Each step has **Back**, **Next**, and **Generate Designs** buttons at the bottom of the panel. After you finish the brand step, click **Generate Designs** to produce optimized, brand-compliant designs for your site.

## After project creation

Once designs are generated, configure setbacks, constraints, and zoning from the project dashboard:

- [Setbacks and adjacency](/working-with-projects/setbacks-adjacency): set required setbacks for each side of your site
- [Constraints](/working-with-projects/constraints-sorting): apply FAR, height, open space, and other design constraints
- [Zoning Panel](/tutorials/zoning-panel): review district codes and apply zoning-derived values

## Start a new project

Start a new project from any of three entry points:

- The **New Project** button on your dashboard
- **Add a New Project** link on the dashboard
- Direct URL: [app.parafin.ai/dashboard/create](https://app.parafin.ai/dashboard/create)
