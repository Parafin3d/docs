---
title: "Enter the site address"
description: "Locate your site by entering a U.S. or Canadian address."
---

{/* Figma source: Project Location https://www.figma.com/design/hevlKEYRL9U8bXkXiKqMky/Parafin?node-id=11327-90614 */}

Enter a street address to place a pin on the map at your site. Parafin supports addresses in the United States and Canada. The project name you entered in the previous step appears at the top of the panel.

<Frame caption="Enter an address to locate your site on the map">
  <img src="/images/help/launching/site-address.png" alt="Location step showing address field with map pin" />
</Frame>

A tooltip on the right side of the screen explains that you can search for an address or zoom in on the map to navigate to your site.

<Note>
  If you don't know the exact address, enter the closest known address and use the map to pan over to the actual site before selecting parcels.
</Note>
