---
title: "Exporting designs"
description: "Download a selected design as PDF or Excel."
---

Click **Download** above the executive summary or from the sort menu to export the selected design. Two formats are available:

- **PDF** — financial results, site plan, and floor plans
- **Excel** — financial results only, for further analysis
