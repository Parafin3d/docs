<Update label="1.61" description="December 30, 2025" tags={["feature", "zoning", "pcf"]}>
  ## Zoning Integration

  We've spent the past several months redesigning core parts of Parafin so zoning data can be integrated intuitively across the United States and Canada. Release 1.61 introduces the first phase of that system and brings zoning directly into your workflow. All zoning capabilities remain free for existing Parafin users throughout this period.

  ### New Zoning Panel

  The new Zoning Panel brings all available zoning information for your site into a single, easy-to-navigate location. Open it by clicking anywhere in the project information section and expanding the Zoning section. You'll see the district code, description, zoning type, and a direct link to the municipal zoning source, followed by the key dimensional controls that influence feasibility.

  <Frame caption="The new Zoning Panel with district code, zoning type, source link, and dimensional controls">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.61/zoning_panel.gif" alt="Walkthrough of the new Zoning Panel inside a project" />
  </Frame>

  [Learn more →](/tutorials/zoning-panel)

  ### Zoning Filters

  Zoning Filters let you apply zoning requirements to your project with a single click. Constraints such as FAR, maximum building height, and minimum open space can be reviewed and applied directly from the zoning panel. Each constraint displays a suggested zoning value — apply it immediately, enter your own value using the text field or slider, or turn zoning controls off entirely for that metric. Activating a filter updates your project assumptions in real time, and your variant generations adjust accordingly.

  <Frame caption="Applying FAR, height, and open-space constraints directly from the zoning panel">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.61/control_filters.gif" alt="Toggling and applying Zoning Filters in the panel" />
  </Frame>

  ### Defining Setbacks with Zoning

  Setbacks are now fully integrated into the zoning workflow. When zoning data is available for your site, Parafin displays suggested zoning values for front, side, and rear setbacks. Apply them directly or adjust using the slider or text field — the site footprint in the viewer responds in real time.

  <Frame caption="Suggested zoning setback values applied to front, side, and rear">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.61/setback_define.gif" alt="Defining setbacks using suggested zoning values" />
  </Frame>

  Assign setback values to the appropriate edges of the site. Each boundary is labeled, and you select which sides correspond to front, rear, and side yards. Parafin then applies the zoning setback values to each designated edge.

  <Frame caption="Assigning labeled setback values to each edge of the site">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.61/setback_sides.gif" alt="Assigning setback values to labeled site edges" />
  </Frame>

  ### Zoning Data Coverage

  Parafin's zoning experience is powered by data sourced from Zoneomics, which aggregates and standardizes zoning and land-use information across the United States and Canada. Zoning data refreshes weekly. Coverage is broad but not universal — if your site doesn't show zoning data, let us know and we'll make sure it's added in the next weekly update.

  <Frame caption="Zoneomics coverage across the United States and Canada">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.61/zoneomics_coverage.jpg" alt="Map of Zoneomics zoning data coverage" />
  </Frame>

  ### Project Creation Progress Bar

  As part of preparing the platform for deeper zoning integration, we've redesigned the project creation flow to be more structured and transparent. This release introduces the first element of that redesign: a new progress bar that clearly shows where you are in the project creation process. It reflects each major step — location, site definition, setbacks, and brand selection — giving you clearer context as you move through setup.

  <Frame caption="New project creation progress bar">
    <img src="/images/releases/1.61/project_progress_bar.png" alt="Project creation progress bar showing each step of setup" />
  </Frame>
</Update>
