---
title: "3D Building Viewer"
description: "Rotate, mirror, and reposition the selected building on its site."
---

The 3D Building Viewer lets you manipulate the selected design in place on the site:

- **Rotate** around the site center
- **Mirror** across a chosen axis
- **Translate** to reposition the building within the site

Property constraints turn red when exceeded, so you see violations immediately as you move the building.
