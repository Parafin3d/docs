---
title: "Adjusting assumptions"
description: "Set revenue, budget, and investment assumptions to update design outputs."
---

Parafin's financial outputs are driven by three groups of assumptions. Adjust any of them, then click **Recalculate** in the top left to refresh the outputs.

## Revenue

Set stabilized-year revenue for guestrooms, meeting rooms, and food & beverage. For each room type, enter ADR and occupancy.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/support/legacy/designs/input-guestroom-revenue.jpg" alt="Revenue assumptions panel" />
</Frame>

## Budget

Enter land acquisition, hard costs, soft costs, FF&E, developer overhead, and project contingency. Additional line items that Parafin doesn't break out separately (OS&E, IT, pre-opening) go into soft costs. Developer overhead and contingency are entered as percentages of the total budget.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/support/legacy/designs/input-budget.jpg" alt="Budget assumptions panel" />
</Frame>

## Investment

Set NOI margin, disposition, and financing. Construction loan interest is included in the project budget. The sale date assumes the first hypothetical stabilized year.
