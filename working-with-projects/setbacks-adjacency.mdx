Configure setback distances and adjacency rules from the project dashboard after creating your project. Each side of the site is labeled independently, and the map outline updates in real time as you adjust values.

## Opening the Setbacks panel

Open the **Setbacks** panel from either entry point:

- The **setbacks button** in the top toolbar
- Clicking anywhere in the **project information side panel**

<Frame caption="Opening the Setbacks panel from the top toolbar">
  <img src="/images/help/working/setbacks-open-toolbar.gif" alt="Opening the Setbacks panel from the toolbar" />
</Frame>

You can also reach the panel from the project information area on the left side of the screen.

<Frame caption="Opening the Setbacks panel from the project information side panel">
  <img src="/images/help/working/setbacks-open-sidepanel.gif" alt="Opening the Setbacks panel from the side panel" />
</Frame>

Both entry points open the same Setbacks panel.

## Adjusting setback distances

Adjust individual setback distances inside the panel. Changes apply to the site footprint in the viewer in real time.

## Simplifying complex parcels

County parcel geometry sometimes produces more sides than you need. Group adjacent sides together to reduce the number of distinct setback labels.

<Frame caption="Grouping adjacent parcel sides to simplify setback labeling">
  <img src="/images/help/working/setbacks-group-sides.gif" alt="Grouping adjacent sides together in the Setbacks panel" />
</Frame>

## Using zoning-suggested setback values

When zoning data is available for your site, Parafin displays suggested zoning values for front, side, and rear setbacks. Apply them as-is or adjust using the slider or text field. The site footprint updates in real time.

<Frame caption="Suggested zoning setback values applied to front, side, and rear">
  <img src="/images/help/working/setbacks-zoning-values.gif" alt="Defining setbacks using suggested zoning values" />
</Frame>

Assign each labeled boundary to a front, rear, or side yard. Parafin applies the matching zoning setback value to each designated edge.

<Frame caption="Assigning labeled setback values to each edge of the site">
  <img src="/images/help/working/setbacks-assign-sides.gif" alt="Assigning setback values to labeled site edges" />
</Frame>

Once all sides are assigned, Parafin applies the correct zoning setback distance to each edge automatically.

For more on zoning data, see the [Zoning Panel tutorial](/tutorials/zoning-panel).
