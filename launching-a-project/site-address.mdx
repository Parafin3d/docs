Enter a street address to drop Parafin at your site. Parafin supports addresses in the United States and Canada.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/support/legacy/launching/project-address.jpg" alt="Site address entry" />
</Frame>

<Note>
  If you don't know the exact address, enter the closest known address and use the map to pan over to the actual site before selecting parcels.
</Note>

## Zoning data coverage

Zoning data is sourced from Zoneomics and refreshed weekly. It covers sites across the United States and Canada. If your site doesn't show zoning data when you open the Zoning Panel, [let us know](mailto:support@parafin.ai) and we'll add it in the next weekly update.
